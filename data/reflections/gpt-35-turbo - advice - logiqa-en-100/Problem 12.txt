Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Identify the conclusion of the argument.
 - Identify the evidence that supports the conclusion.
 - Identify the assumption that links the evidence to the conclusion.
 - Evaluate each option to determine which one is the correct assumption.
 - Choose the option that best supports the conclusion based on the evidence provided.
 - Be careful not to confuse evidence with assumptions.
