Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the argument.
 - Identify the main point of the argument and the evidence used to support it.
 - Identify the assumptions made in the argument.
 - Evaluate the validity of the evidence and assumptions.
 - Choose the answer that best questions the validity of the conclusion.
 - Double-check your answer to ensure it addresses the main point of the argument.
 - Remember that the best question is the one that challenges the validity of the conclusion.
