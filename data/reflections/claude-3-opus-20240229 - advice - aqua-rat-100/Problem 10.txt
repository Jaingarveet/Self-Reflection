Advice:
 - Always read the problem statement carefully and understand the context.
 - Identify the key information given and what you need to solve for.
 - Break down the problem into smaller, manageable steps.
 - Make reasonable assumptions when necessary, but state them clearly.
 - Double-check your calculations and ensure your answer makes sense in the context of the problem.
 - If your calculated answer doesn't exactly match an answer choice, choose the closest one.
 - Practice translating word problems into mathematical equations and vice versa.
