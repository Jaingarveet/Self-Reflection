Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem before attempting to solve it.
 - Identify the key information and conditions given in the problem.
 - Assign variables to unknown quantities and write equations based on the given conditions.
 - When solving a system of equations, substitute one equation into the other to eliminate one variable.
 - Always simplify equations step-by-step and show your work.
 - Double-check your calculations at each step to catch any errors early on.
 - Verify that your final answer makes sense in the context of the problem.
