Advice:

* Always read the problem carefully and understand the given information.
* Always break down complex problems into smaller, manageable steps.
* Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
* Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
* Always double-check your calculations and final answer.
* Remember that division is the inverse operation of multiplication, so when dividing a number by another number, you are essentially multiplying by the reciprocal of the divisor.
