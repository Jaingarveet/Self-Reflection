Advice:

* Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
* Always break down complex problems into smaller, manageable steps.
* Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
* Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
* Always double-check your calculations and final answer.
* Remember that percentage problems often require a calculation of the part of a whole, which can be done by multiplying the whole by the percentage.
