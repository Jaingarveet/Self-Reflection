Instructions:
1. Identify the origin of the facial artery (external carotid).
2. Trace the course of the facial artery along the face.
3. Identify the named branches of the facial artery.
4. Follow the terminal branch of the facial artery (angular artery).
5. Determine what the angular artery anastomoses with at its terminal end.
6. Identify the origin of the artery that the angular artery anastomoses with (ophthalmic artery from internal carotid).
