Advice:
- Be careful not to assume that something true of some members of a group is true of all members of that group.
- Be careful not to assume that something true of a group is true of all the individual members of that group.
- Be careful not to assume that something true of an individual is true of all the groups to which that individual belongs.
- Be careful not to assume that something true of an individual is true of all the other individuals who share some characteristic with that individual.
