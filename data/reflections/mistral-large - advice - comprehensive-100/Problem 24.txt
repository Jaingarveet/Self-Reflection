Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
 - Always double-check your reasoning and final answer.
 - Be aware of any assumptions you are making and check them against the conditions of the problem.
 - Be careful not to misinterpret the conditions of the problem.
 - Consider all possible options and eliminate any that contradict the conditions.
 - Remember that the conditions for each student's report may affect the options for other students' reports.
