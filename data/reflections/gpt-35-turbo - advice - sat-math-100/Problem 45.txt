Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always use the given data to find the slope and y-intercept of a linear function.
 - Always double-check your calculations and final answer.
 - Remember that the slope of a linear function is the change in y divided by the change in x.
 - Remember that the y-intercept of a linear function is the y-value when x is 0.
