Advice:
 - Always consider the specific context of the patient when making medication recommendations.
 - Always consult reliable sources of drug information, such as textbooks, peer-reviewed journals, and reputable online resources.
 - Always prioritize patient safety when making medication recommendations.
 - Be aware of the potential risks and benefits of all medications, especially during pregnancy.
 - When in doubt, consult with a healthcare professional with expertise in the area.
