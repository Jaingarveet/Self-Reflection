Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
 - Always double-check your answer to ensure accuracy.
 - Remember to consider all relevant factors when evaluating answer choices.
 - Do not overemphasize one factor at the expense of others.
 - Be aware of the potential for misinterpretation of the question.
 - Always choose the answer that most directly supports the prediction.
