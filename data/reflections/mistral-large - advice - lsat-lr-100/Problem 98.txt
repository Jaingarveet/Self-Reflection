Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always identify the assumption that the argument is based on.
 - Always evaluate the validity of the assumption.
 - Always identify the specific assumption that the argument takes for granted.
 - Always evaluate the argument's vulnerability to criticism based on the assumption.
 - Always choose the answer that best describes the assumption that the argument is most vulnerable to criticism on.
 - Remember that an argument can be vulnerable to criticism on multiple grounds, but the question asks for the assumption that the argument is most vulnerable to criticism on.
