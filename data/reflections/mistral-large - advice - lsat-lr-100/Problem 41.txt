Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
 - Always double-check your analysis and final answer.
 - Always identify the argument's assumption and evaluate its validity.
 - Always consider alternative possibilities and evaluate their relevance.
 - Always evaluate whether the argument's conclusion follows logically from its premises.
 - Always analyze the argument's structure and reasoning.
 - Always be aware of common logical fallacies and biases.
