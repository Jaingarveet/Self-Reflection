Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always identify the premise and conclusion of the argument.
 - Always determine the logical relationship between the premise and the conclusion.
 - Always evaluate each answer choice carefully and choose the one that represents the assumption.
 - Never make assumptions that are not supported by the information given in the argument.
 - Remember that the assumption is the unstated premise that must be true for the conclusion to follow logically from the premise.
