Advice:
 - Always read the problem and conditions very carefully.
 - Break down complex problems into clear, simple conditions.
 - Check each condition against each answer choice methodically.
 - Be careful not to misinterpret or misapply the conditions.
 - Eliminate choices that violate any condition; the correct answer must satisfy all conditions.
 - If you're unsure, re-read the problem and think through your reasoning step-by-step.
 - Don't make assumptions; only use the information given in the problem.
