Advice:
 - Always fully grasp the problem conditions before attempting a solution.
 - Consider each constraint separately and in combination.
 - Systematically evaluate each answer choice against all constraints.
 - Be thorough in your analysis; don't make assumptions.
 - If a choice seems impossible, double-check your reasoning.
 - Eliminate choices through the process of contradiction.
 - The one choice that cannot be true will be the correct answer in an EXCEPT question.
