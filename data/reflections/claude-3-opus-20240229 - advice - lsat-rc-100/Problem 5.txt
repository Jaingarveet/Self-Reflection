Advice:
 - Always read passages carefully, taking note of the main ideas and how they are developed.
 - Pay attention to the level of detail given to each idea to understand their relative importance.
 - Look for direct comparisons or contrasts between ideas, as these often indicate the passage's structure.
 - Don't assume ideas are elaborated equally; some may be explained in more depth than others.
 - Make sure your answer choice accurately reflects the structure you've identified in the passage.
