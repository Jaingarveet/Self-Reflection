Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Identify the conclusion that needs to be drawn.
 - Identify the evidence provided in the question.
 - Identify the assumption needed to draw the conclusion.
 - Evaluate each answer choice against the assumption.
 - Choose the answer choice that best supports the assumption and enables the conclusion to be properly drawn.
 - Be careful not to choose an answer choice that is too specific or too narrow.
