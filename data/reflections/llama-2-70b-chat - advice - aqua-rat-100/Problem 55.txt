Advice:

* Always read the question carefully and understand what is being asked.
* Always decompose complex problems into simpler steps.
* Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
* Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
* Always double-check your calculations and final answer.
* Remember that the rate along the current is found by subtracting the rate against the current from the rate in still water.
