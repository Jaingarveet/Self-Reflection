Advice:
 - Always read the instructions carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
 - Always double-check your calculations and final answer.
 - Remember that a perfect square is a number that can be obtained by squaring an integer.
 - Remember that the sum of an arithmetic series can be calculated using the formula: sum = (n/2)*(a + l), where n is the number of terms, a is the first term, and l is the last term.
