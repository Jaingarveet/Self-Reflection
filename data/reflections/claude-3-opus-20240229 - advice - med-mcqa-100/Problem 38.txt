Advice:
 - Familiarize yourself with common radiologic signs and their associated conditions.
 - When answering questions about radiologic findings, visualize the appearance of each condition.
 - Eliminate answer choices that do not match the key radiologic finding mentioned in the question.
 - If unsure, use the process of elimination to narrow down the possible correct answers.
 - Remember that some radiologic signs are highly specific for certain conditions, while others may be more nonspecific.
