Advice:
 - Always consider the key diagnostic features of a condition when selecting appropriate investigations.
 - Understand the strengths and limitations of different imaging modalities and views.
 - Differentiate between the perspectives provided by different imaging views (e.g., AP vs. lateral).
 - Eliminate investigations that do not effectively contribute to the diagnosis of the specific condition.
 - Stay updated with the latest guidelines and recommendations for diagnostic imaging in various conditions.
