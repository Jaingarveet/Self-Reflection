 Explanation:

Error Keywords:

 - Incorrect reasoning
Solution:
The number of friends who attended the picnic was initially 8. Since 4 of them did not turn up, the number of friends who attended the picnic became 8 - 4 = 4.
The remaining friends had to contribute Rs. 4 extra, each.
The correct answer is (A) 8.
Instructions:
1. Identify the initial number of friends who attended the picnic.
2. Calculate the number of friends who did not turn up.
3. Subtract the number of friends who did not turn up from the initial number of friends.
4. Determine the amount each friend had to contribute extra.
5. Choose the answer that matches the correct calculation.
