Advice:

* Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
* Always organize the information in a clear and concise way.
* Always analyze the information and identify relationships between elements.
* Always look for contradictions or inconsistencies in the information.
* Always eliminate answer choices that are dependent on incorrect assumptions.
* Always choose the answer that is most strongly supported by the information.
* Remember that the correct answer may not always be the most obvious one.
