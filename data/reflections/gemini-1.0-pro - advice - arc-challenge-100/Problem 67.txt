Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand what it is asking for.
 - Consider all possible answers that could be correct.
 - Think about the specific context of the question.
 - Choose the answer that best fits the question and the context.
 - If multiple answers are possible, select all that apply.
 - Don't assume that there is only one correct answer.
 - Be aware of your own biases and assumptions.
 - Be willing to revise your answer if you find new information.
