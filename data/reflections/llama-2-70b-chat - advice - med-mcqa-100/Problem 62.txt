Additional Tips:

* The interstitial part of the uterine tube is the narrowest part, located between the ampulla and the isthmus.
* The isthmus is the wider part of the uterine tube, connecting the ampulla to the uterus.
* The infundibulum is the funnel-shaped opening of the uterine tube that connects to the ovary.
* The ampulla is the wider part of the uterine tube that receives the ovum from the infundibulum.
