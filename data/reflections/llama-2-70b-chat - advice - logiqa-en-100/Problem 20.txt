Advice:

* Always read the passage carefully and understand the argument made.
* Identify the assumption upon which the argument relies.
* Evaluate the assumption and determine whether it is valid.
* Determine whether the conclusion logically follows from the assumption.
* Consider alternative perspectives and evaluate their implications.
* Be aware of your own biases and try to approach the argument objectively.
* Consider multiple possible interpretations of the argument and evaluate their implications.
