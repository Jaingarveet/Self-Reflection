Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
 - Always double-check your logical reasoning and final answer.
 - Remember that a necessary condition is a requirement for the conclusion to be true, while a sufficient condition guarantees the conclusion to be true.
 - Consider all possible conditions that could lead to the conclusion, not just the ones mentioned in the premise.
