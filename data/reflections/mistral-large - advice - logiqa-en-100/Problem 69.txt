Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always identify the inference being made in the question.
 - Always identify the assumptions that are given in the question.
 - Always determine which assumption contradicts the inference.
 - Always read each answer choice carefully and evaluate whether it contradicts the inference.
 - Always choose the answer that does not contradict the inference.
 - Remember that assumptions are not necessarily true, but are used to make an inference.
 - Remember that the correct answer may not contradict the inference, but rather be consistent with it.
