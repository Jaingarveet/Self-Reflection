Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
 - Always double-check your evaluation of each option and final answer.
 - Remember that the rules must be satisfied for all options, not just some.
 - Be careful not to make assumptions about the options; evaluate each option based on the rules.
