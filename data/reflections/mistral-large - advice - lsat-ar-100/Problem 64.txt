Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never make assumptions that are not supported by the given information.
 - Always consider all possible distributions of elements in the problem.
 - Always double-check your reasoning and final answer.
 - Remember that logical reasoning is a critical skill in law and many other fields.
