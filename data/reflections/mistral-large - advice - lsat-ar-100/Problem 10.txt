Advice:
 - Always read the problem carefully and understand the requirements.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
 - Always double-check your answer to ensure that it meets all the requirements.
 - Do not make assumptions about the problem that are not explicitly stated.
 - Always eliminate options that do not meet the requirements before making a final decision.
