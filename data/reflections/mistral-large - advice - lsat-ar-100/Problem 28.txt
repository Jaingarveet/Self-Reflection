Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never skip any steps; be explicit in each step of your reasoning.
 - Always double-check your assumptions and final answer.
 - Remember to consider all the conditions and their implications.
 - Be aware of the interdependencies between the conditions.
 - Do not make assumptions that are not supported by the information given.
 - Do not overlook crucial details in the question.
 - Do not misinterpret conditional statements.
