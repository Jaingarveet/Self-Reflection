Advice:
 - Always read the question carefully and understand the problem.
 - Always decompose complex problems into multiple simple steps.
 - Always think through each subproblem step-by-step.
 - Never make assumptions that are not supported by the information given.
 - Always consider all possible scenarios.
 - Always use logical reasoning to eliminate impossible scenarios.
 - Always double-check your reasoning and final answer.
 - Remember that each student can give more than one report.
 - Remember that the conditions of the problem may limit when and where each student can give a report.
