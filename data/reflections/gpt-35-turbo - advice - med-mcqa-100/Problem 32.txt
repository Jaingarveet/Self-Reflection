Advice:
 - Always gather all the information about the patient's symptoms, medical history, and biopsy findings.
 - Always consider all the possible causes of the patient's symptoms.
 - Always evaluate each possible diagnosis based on the patient's symptoms and biopsy findings.
 - Never rush to a diagnosis without considering all the possible causes.
 - Always double-check your diagnosis to ensure accuracy.
 - Remember that some diseases have similar symptoms, and a biopsy may be necessary to confirm the diagnosis.
