$$x = \frac{-12 \pm \sqrt{(12)^2 - 4 \cdot 3 \cdot 6}}{2 \cdot 3} = \frac{-12 \pm \sqrt{144 - 72}}{6} = \frac{-12 \pm 12\sqrt{30}}{36}$$

Simplifying further, we get:

$$x = \frac{-1 \pm \sqrt{30}}{3}$$
