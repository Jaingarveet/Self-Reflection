2. **I on Middle Shelf:** I is always on the middle shelf.
3. **K above F:** K needs to be above F, but this doesn't restrict K to the top shelf.

6. **Bottom Shelf Capacity:** Since more books are on the bottom shelf than the top, and G is alone on top, the bottom shelf must have at least 3 books.

*   **Top Shelf:** G
