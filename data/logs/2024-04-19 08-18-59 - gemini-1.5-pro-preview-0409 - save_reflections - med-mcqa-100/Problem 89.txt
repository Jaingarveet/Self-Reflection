4.  **CT scan offers detailed bony structures:** This imaging modality provides a 3D visualization of the vertebrae and can be helpful in complex cases or surgical planning.
5.  **MRI visualizes soft tissues:** While not the primary tool for diagnosing spondylolisthesis, MRI can be useful in assessing nerve compression or disc involvement, which may accompany the condition.
