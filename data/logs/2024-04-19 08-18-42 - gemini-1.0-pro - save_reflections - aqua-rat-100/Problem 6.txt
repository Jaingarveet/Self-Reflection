* **Incomplete work calculation:** I failed to account for the remaining work after Mr. Brown's departure.
* **Incorrect time allocation:** I miscalculated the time Mr. Blue had to complete the remaining work.
## Solution:
1. **Calculate individual work:**
    * Mr. Brown: 8 days / 6 days/house = 4/3 houses
    * Mr. Black: 6 days / 8 days/house = 3/4 houses
2. **Calculate combined work:**
    * Overlapping period: 6 days
    * Combined work during overlap: (4/3 houses/day) * 6 days + (3/4 houses/day) * 6 days = 5 houses
3. **Calculate remaining work:**
    * Total houses: 3 houses
    * Work completed: 5 houses
    * Remaining work: 3 houses - 5 houses = -2 houses 
4. **Adjust for negative work:**
    * Since the remaining work is negative, it indicates that the combined work has exceeded the required amount. 
    * This means Mr. Blue doesn't need to work any further.
5. **Answer:** 
    * Mr. Blue needs 0 days to complete the contract.
